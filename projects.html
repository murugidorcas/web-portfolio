Hobbo

But then along the way, I realized that people could have more in common than just hobbies and interests, so I'm working towards integrating more than just those two factors. My initial thought was a web app, but then I felt like creating the web equivalent of the same app. So any sort of progress I make, I ensure it is two-way (web and mobile).
